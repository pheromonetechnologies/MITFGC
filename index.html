OUR MISSION

Code is the new currency. At PHEROMONE, we believe in the power of Open Source. We are a collective of builders, designers, and problem-solvers who learn by shipping real projects.

What we do

  • Weekly hands-on workshops covering web, systems, and hardware basics.
  • Project sprints where small teams build, iterate, and demo.
  • Mentorship circles pairing new members with experienced contributors.
  • Community showcases for feedback and portfolio-ready demos.

WHY JOIN PHEROMONE

Join a community where learning is hands-on, mentorship is real, and projects ship.

  • Learn by building: practical projects that help you grow.
  • Real mentorship: seniors and alumni guide your first steps.
  • Showcase your work: projects you can include in your portfolio.
  • Collaborative culture: teammates who help you finish what you start.

GET STARTED

Ready to join? Follow these simple steps:

  1. Attend our weekly meeting — drop in and say hello.
  2. Pick a project or suggest an idea.
  3. Find a mentor and start contributing.
  4. Present at demo night and celebrate progress.

Email us at join@pheromone.club to get the meeting link.
